Command Manual for OpenSees Thermal

This command manual is specially developed for modelling 'Structures in fire' in OpenSees. The work refers to the PhD projects of Dr. Jian Jiang, Dr. Yaqiang Jiang, Dr. Jian Zhang, Dr. Liming Jiang.

Thermo-mechanical analysis

  • Thermo-mechanical Material
    • Uniaxial materials: Steel01Thermal | Steel02Thermal | SteelECThermal | Concrete02Thermal | ConcreteECThermal | ElasticThermal
    nD materials: ElasticIsotropicThermal | J2PlaneStressThermal | CDPPlaneStressThermal
  • Thermal-mechanical Section
    • Beam Section: FiberSecThermal | FiberSectionGJThermal
    Shell Section: MembraneShellThermal | LayeredShellThermal
  • Thermal-mechanical Element
    • BeamColumn: DispBeamColumnThermal | ForceBeamColumnThermal
    Shell: ShellMITC4Thermal | ShellNLDKGQThermal
  • Thermal Action
    • Beam2DThermalAction | Beam3dThermalAction | NodalThermalAction | ThermalActionWrapper | Pattern: FireLoadPattern
