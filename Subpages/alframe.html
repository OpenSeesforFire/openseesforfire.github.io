Demonstration of column pull in action with a scaled down 10 storey frame model

Introduction

Figure E.6-1 shows a 10 building model with storey height of 50mm and beam span of 250mm. The cross sections of the beam and column are 25 X 2 mm and 25 X 1mm made with Aluminium. An uniform loading of 0.05N/mm has been applied over the beams and a point load of 200N applied over the column. Floors 4,5 and 6 have been subjected to a thermal action of 6500C applied linearly.

Loading and boundary conditions

Figure E.6-1: A 10 storey building frame model

The beams of the frame are assumed to be spanning between strong central core and outer peripheral columns. Therefore the right support is extremely rigid and left support is provided by the columns.
